# AI Monitoring and Review Controls

## Purpose

This document defines monitoring controls for AI use cases after approval.

## Monitoring areas

| Area | Monitoring question | Evidence |
|---|---|---|
| Output quality | Are outputs accurate, complete, and appropriate? | Sample review log |
| Data quality | Are source data issues affecting AI outputs? | Data quality results |
| Human oversight | Are users reviewing outputs before action? | Approval records |
| Privacy | Are sensitive data controls working? | Privacy review records |
| Security | Are access and usage logs reviewed? | Security logs |
| Bias/fairness | Are outputs creating inappropriate variation across groups? | Fairness review |
| Vendor | Has the vendor changed terms, models, or subprocessors? | Vendor review |
| Scope | Is the AI use still within the approved purpose? | Use-case review |

## Review frequency
- Low risk: Annual
- Medium risk: Quarterly
- High risk: Monthly or before each major release

## Escalation

Material issues should be logged in the AI issue and incident log and escalated to the business owner, data steward, privacy/security lead, and AI governance lead as appropriate.
